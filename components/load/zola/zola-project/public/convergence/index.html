We bring the power of open source and customer community to you.

Red Hat Convergence is a complimentary one-day event and online webinar series for Red Hat customers to learn from and collaborate with the experts at the heart of the applications that your business runs on. This is a unique opportunity for you to ask your questions directly to the Red Hat engineers who create and work on the solutions you use every day, in a small group setting.

Reimagined for 2021 + 2022, we will host a series of webinar sessions showcasing Red Hat's product portfolio.

Highlights include:​

  • Panel discussions
  • Interactive Q&A sessions
  • Opportunities to provide feedback on Red Hat products and services
  • Live lab and training guides
  • Products roadmaps, demos, and much more
Tweets by RedHatSupport

Who attends?

Red Hat Convergence gives you the opportunity to network with other people and companies in your industry.

We customize these events specifically for system administrators, engineers, architects, and developers. Whether you’re an administrator looking for hands-on training or an engineer seeking insight about Red Hat’s technology roadmap, there’s something for every level of interest and need.

Each event agenda is customized to fit the needs of the attending customers. Check out the events tab to see the upcoming schedule. To request topics for a Red Hat Convergence event, contact your Technical Account Manager, Customer Success Manager, or Red Hat Account Team.

  • Customer panels on using open source technology
  • Success stories from system administrators on Red Hat Insights
  • Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform: holistic IT automation
  • Red Hat Satellite: Improve how you manage your Red Hat infrastructure
  • Red Hat Enterprise Linux: migration strategies and performance tuning
  • Red Hat Identity Management and Infrastructure
  • Red Hat Enterprise Linux security, identity, and web console
  • Scale, bridge, and gain insights: Red Hat Data Services
  • Hands-on lab experience with RHEL: the intelligent OS
  • Driving workloads to your platform with Red Hat OpenShift
  • Scale your applications with Red Hat Advanced Cluster Management
  • Red Hat OpenShift: what's new and what's next
  • Much more...
