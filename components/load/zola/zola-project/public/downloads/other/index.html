Liberation Fonts

Red Hat announced the public release of a family of fonts under the trademark LIBERATION at the 2007 Red Hat Summit. The fonts are available in TrueType format and within three sets: Sans, Serif, and Mono. You are free to use these fonts on any system you would like. This was just one way for Red Hat to say thank you to all our friends in the open source community for all you have done to make us successful.
